His mother died when he was younger of an overdose, and his father worked all the time.

　　 The truth is, the reason why he got closer to his uncle to begin with was because his own father was mostly absent from his life, constantly delaying responsibilities to his son. He didn't mean any harm, and thought Theo old enough to be able to handle it all perfectly. Alas, he was mistaken : he was just a young boy, and all of this burden brought him to act out. And ultimately, he got his uncle's attention, who taught him to be what he called a 'successful' man. Meaning, a violent and intolerant man.

　　 He went about his life with a sense of entitlement and superiority that got worse whenever his uncle was around, as he wanted to make him proud : to show him that he, too, could be a /real/ man. One night, however, Alexander (his uncle and Beau's father) spotted him outside a club, completely drunk and making out with another man. All Theodore remembered then, was that he woke up at the hospital, having lost sight in one eye because of the violence of his uncle's punches. He then visited him, reassuring him that he would 'fix' his deviant ways. To do so, he hired prostitute after prostitute and forced him to engage in the same amount of violence he had whenever encountering a man Alexander deemed 'unworthy'.

　　 After a while, it became a second nature to him and he would act as taught unprovoked, and without Alexander's presence. He abandoned his dreams of being a model, as this too was deemed to be a 'girl's thing' and became a mechanic instead, like his uncle.

　　 When the latter died, Theodore moved to the US to find his cousin and fix him as his uncle had him.
